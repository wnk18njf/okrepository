What is caving!? Some FAQ's...

So you're here, which probably means you're wondering just what this caving business is!? Here are some frequently asked questions.

What is caving?

As stated on www.trycaving.co.uk, caving is the recreational exploration of caves and potholes! A typical caving trip may involve climbing, abseiling, crawling, swimming, and walking. Every cave is different, they vary massively in terms of size and shape, some chambers are big enough to fit a cathedral, whereas some are small enough that cavers may need to crawl on their bellies!

What do you wear?

Typical caving equipment consists of:

  • A cave suit (theyre pretty much all the same!)
  • Wellies!
  • Wet Socks (as in wet suit socks)
  • A helmet with a headtorch
  • A caving belt
  • Kneepads(your best friend..)
