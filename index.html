About Us

Re-established in 2020, Bangor Students Caving Club(BSCC) offers its members the chance to see places many dont get the chance.

We run weekly trips to local caves and mines, as well as trips further afield every 6 weeks, to places such as the Mendips, South Wales, and Yorkshire!
